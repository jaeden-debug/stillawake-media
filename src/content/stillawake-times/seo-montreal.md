SEO helps Montreal businesses generate long-term traffic, visibility, and leads without relying entirely on paid advertising.

Local SEO helps businesses appear in location-based searches and Google Maps results.

Technical SEO focuses on crawlability, indexing, site structure, page speed, mobile optimization, and search engine accessibility.

SEO is a long-term strategy. Results often improve progressively over several months depending on competition and site quality.

Internal linking helps search engines understand relationships between pages and improves crawl efficiency.

Topical authority refers to how comprehensively a website covers a subject area.

## Does mobile optimization matter for SEO?

Absolutely. Google indexes mobile-first and evaluates mobile usability heavily.

## What is structured data?

Structured data helps search engines better understand page content and relationships.

Yes. Strong SEO attracts highly relevant visitors actively searching for solutions.
